## Postgres (Arch)

* Connect as postgres user: `$ sudo -iu postgres`
  * Create new DB: `$[postgres] createdb dbname`
  * Connect to specific DB: `$[postgres] psql -d dbname`
  * Drop db: `$[postgres] dropdb dbname`
